# Ubundi

**Infrastructure for AI agents that remember.**

[ubundi.com](https://ubundi.com) &nbsp;·&nbsp; [Open source](#open-source)

</div>

---

Ubundi builds long-term memory, retrieval, and agent-tooling infrastructure for
AI agents — plus the operational and Physical AI systems around them. This is
our public surface: open-source tools, benchmarks, plugins, and docs.
